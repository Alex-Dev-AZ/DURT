MISSION STATEMENT // ESTABLISHING COMMS...

We're a group of high schoolers who founded the Delta Underwater Robotics Team (DURT) in the spirit of building things from scratch. We compete independently, with no school backing and no shortcuts.

At our last competition, we earned a Judges' Award as one of the few teams to build our robot from scratch. That's who we are, and that's how we'll show up to the 2026 National Underwater Robotics Competition (NURC) hosted by ASU.
