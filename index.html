Who We Are

Founded in 2025, DURT is a student-led robotics program. We are a diverse group of programmers, builders, and designers dedicated to exploring the world of STEM through competitive robotics.

What We're Competing For

This year, we are taking on NURC 2026. Our goal is to engineer an ROV capable of performing tasks and manuvers. We aren't just competing for trophies; we're competing to refine our skills in CAD, C++, and strategic planning.
